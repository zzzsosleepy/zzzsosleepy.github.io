Vinny

[Vocals, guitar, synthesizer, keyboard]

Primary songwriter and singer for Red Vox, Vinny also plays most of the rhythm guitars and some leads. Like the rest of Red Vox, he was born and raised on Staten Island, NY, and as such has an affinity for rats and pizza, though not in that order.

Joe

[Bass, guitar, studio production]

Joe has recorded, engineered and produced every Red Vox record so far. He was the frontman of Happy Anarchy and can now be found playing the lead guitar parts and most bass in Red Vox. Anytime you hear a solo that is complicated and articulate, it's likely Joe. Take that Vin.

Mike

[Percussion]

Hits things with sticks as most drummers tend to do. Influenced by the likes of Keith Moon of The Who, John Bonham of Led Zeppelin, Animal from The Muppets and Gizmo from Gremlins, Mike is drums.

Bill

[Keyboard, piano, bass]

When the moon is right, Bill will come into the studio and play immaculate piano and synth parts, usually in just a few takes. He not only plays most instruments, but he has fronted many a band in his day. A useful addition to any recipe.

Jerrold

[Bass (Live), Studio Collaborator]

Bassist for live shows and part-time studio collaborator, Jerrold has contributed much to the recent Red Vox catalogue. You can find him as InnerMotionMusic covering video game music classics on his free time.
